Privacy Policy - Flight Tracker

Last updated: 2026-06-25

Data we collect

Flight Tracker stores the flight information users add to their account, including routes, airport codes, airline names, seat class, purpose, flight date, distance, duration, and related flight statistics.

The app also uses email and password login so users can access their own private account.

How data is used

User data is used only to provide the app's flight tracking features, including saving flights, importing flights, exporting flights, and generating personal statistics.

Data sharing

We do not sell user data. User flight data is not public and is only available to the signed-in account.

Account deletion

Users can delete their account inside the app from the Account tab using the Delete account button.

Deleting an account permanently removes the user's account and saved flights.
