## Description

Flight Tracker helps you keep a personal record of the flights you have taken.

Add flights manually, import your existing flight history from Excel, and view useful statistics about your journeys. The app calculates distance and estimated flight time automatically, so you only need to enter the origin and destination.

Flight Tracker is designed for travellers, aviation fans, frequent flyers, and anyone who wants a simple way to keep their flight history organised.

Features:
- Add flights manually
- Search airports by city name or airport code
- Import flights from Excel
- Export your flight history to Excel
- View total flights, distance, and flight time
- Explore top airports, airlines, routes, seat classes, and trip purposes
- View route maps
- Keep your data private in your own account
- Delete your account and saved flights from inside the app

Your flight history starts empty and belongs to you. No sample users, no public profiles, and no unnecessary clutter.
